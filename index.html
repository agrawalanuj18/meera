Welcome to MEERRA, where children's clothing becomes a canvas for storytelling and cultural heritage.

We're inspired by the beauty of India's craftsmanship-think luxurious muslin, playful hand crochet, and vibrant fabrics dyed with Ayurvedic goodness like madder, turmeric, and indigo. We wanted to create modern clothing that tells a story-one of tradition, artistry, and a connection to the earth-so that you can feel the love and intention behind every stitch.
